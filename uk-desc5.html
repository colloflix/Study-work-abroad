London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine | LSHTM

LSHTM is a constituent affiliated college to the University of London that is specialized in tropical medicine and public health. Its main goal is to participate in the development and advancement of health across the globe by providing high quality teaching, research, and advanced education in health and medicine. Since its establishment, the university has been classified among the highly ranked foundations worldwide when it comes to infectious diseases and public health. In 2009, the LSHTM won the Gates Award for Global Health and received a prize of 1 million dollars.

GSK Scholarships

The London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine scholarships are available to African students who want to study a one-year, full-time program at the London School. The scholarship program aims to financially support the training and development of the next generation of global health leaders.

  • Host University: London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine
  • Host Country: UK
  • Study Level: Masters
  • Eligible Countries: African Students
  • Subjects Available: Medicine
  • Application Deadline: March 1, 2023

Eligibility Criteria

  • The applicant must be nationals of, and resident in, countries in sub-Saharan Africa.
  • The candidates must confirm that they would not otherwise be able to pay for the proposed program of study by submitting the form of the supplementary question.
  • The applicant must meet LSHTM’s minimum English language requirements.
  • The applicant must hold a first degree at either a first or upper second class equivalency level.
  • The applicant must hold an offer of admission for the 2023-24 academic year for one of LSHTM’s London-based MSc programs of study.

Application Procedures

The application process has two steps:

  1. Interested students must apply for a London-based MSc program of study, as per instructions under the ‘How to Apply’ tab on the relevant program of study page before the Application Deadline in March 1, 2023.
  2. Submit an online scholarship application and selecting “2023-24 GSK Scholarships for Future Health Leaders”.
