University of Bedfordshire Scholarship for Undergraduate Students

The University of Bedfordshire has been providing high-quality education for over 100 years. With campuses in Luton, Bedford, Milton Keynes and Aylesbury, it accepts students from various countries and cultures. The university encourages students to become educated, employed, and successful global citizens.

The Foundation Year Undergraduate Scholarship is available for UK and European students in all subjects taught at The University of Bedfordshire.

Scholarship Details

  • Host University: University of Bedfordshire
  • Host Country: United Kingdom
  • Study Level: Undergraduate
  • Scholarship Type: Partial Funding
  • Scholarship Worth: £2,000
  • Eligible Countries: UK and European Students
  • Available Subjects: All Subjects
  • Application Deadline: Open

Scholarship Benefits

The University of Bedfordshire awards a £2000 merit scholarship over 3 academic years to students who earn 112 UCAS points or more. Students who do not qualify for the merit scholarship will receive £1,500 over 3 academic years or £1,000 over 2 academic years if they are an additional student receiving the Level 5 scholarship.

Eligible students need not worry about their financial situation as there are a variety of scholarships available at the University of Bedfordshire to help with their studies, and these are not required to be paid back.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the scholarship, applicants must:

  • be enrolled on a complete, undergraduate, bachelor’s level degree program;
  • be a first-time student starting at the university in the academic year 2023/24 and not already registered at the University of Bedfordshire;
  • be enrolled in the program on the appropriate course days as determined by the award qualification;
  • be a student from the UK/EU that will pay the maximum tuition cost up to £9,250;
  • have completed all necessary financial procedures with sponsors or other external agencies that pay for the expenses.

Application Process

To apply, interested students must register for an eligible program at the University of Bedfordshire before the deadline. Once approved, the application will be automatically processed for this scholarship.
