University of Mississippi International Students Scholarships 2023/24 for Undergraduate Studies in the USA

Are you an international student looking for funding for an undergraduate study in the United States of America? Are you in search of a reputable institution of higher learning recognized internationally where you can be trained? Then apply for the University of Mississippi International Students Scholarships 2023.

The Scholarships are generally offered to deserving students on the basis of a student’s academic merit. Students may be awarded the scholarship for a total of 4 academic years towards a first bachelor’s degree.

Scholarship Details

  • Scholarship Sponsor(s): University of Mississippi
  • Scholarship Type: Tuition Award
  • Host Institution(s): The University of Mississippi
  • Scholarship Worth: Up to $23,454
  • Number of Awards: Several
  • Study Level: Undergraduate
  • Nationality: International Students

Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the International Students Scholarships at 2023/24, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be classified as a foreign student for tuition fee purpose.
  • Must have obtained relevant certificate required for admission into a United States’ institution of higher learning.
  • Must demonstrate excellent academic and leadership abilities.
  • Must be proficient in the use of written and spoken English.
  • Must be available to begin the 2023/24 academic session if successful.

Application Procedures

How-to-Apply: Interested applicants for the University of Mississippi International Students Scholarships 2023/24 are to apply for admission into an eligible undergraduate course at the university

Application Deadline: Not specified
