Newcastle University - Your Path to Success

Newcastle students are provided with world-leading academic courses, supportive and inspiring educational surroundings, and the opportunity to transform conceptual knowledge to action through practical work experience. They have the advantage of learning alongside highly motivated and experienced faculty who have extensive connections and experience in the industry.

The University of Newcastle has high-quality housing for students on campus, suitable for undergraduates, postgraduates, partners and students with special needs. Winners of the Asia Pacific Student Housing Association Award for Excellence in Student Experience, the Student Life Team operates a 24-hour assistance program and offers a comprehensive program to ensure that students are comfortable, welcome and supported throughout their time at Newcastle University.

Newcastle University has developed extensive collaborations with government and industry to stimulate innovation in domains of national and international importance.

Scholarship Description

Host University: University of Newcastle

Host Country: Australia

Study Level: Graduate and Undergraduate Level

Scholarship Type: Partial Funding

Scholarship Worth: Check the details below

Eligible Countries: International Students

Available Subjects: All Subjects

Application Deadline: The Scholarship is open

The CIE Program Scholarships are available for International Students in all fields to have the opportunity to get an Undergraduate or Graduate degree at the University of Newcastle, Australia.

Newcastle University will give successful applicants a 10-20% reduction of the combined tuition fees for the program being pursued.

Eligibility Requirements

In order to be considered for admission, candidates are required to have successfully processed their application and achieved good grades in high school.

Application Process

In order to apply, applicants should access the university’s direct application portal. These grants are automatically attributed and do not necessitate a specific application.

Supporting Documents

The following documents must be submitted to the university:

  • A transcript
  • A certificate of completion
  • Proof of English language proficiency
  • A biometric page of the passport
  • For programs requiring work experience, a curriculum vitae or CV and letters of work experience if available

All of these documents are recommended to apply for the scholarship.

Admission Requirements

It is necessary for candidates to have obtained a secondary school diploma or to possess or be expected to possess a relevant bachelor’s degree.
It is possible for candidates to submit test results and evidence of their English language proficiency in accordance with the university’s English language proficiency requirements.
